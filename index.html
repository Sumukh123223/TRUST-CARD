How It Works

Five Simple Steps to Pay with Crypto

01

Connect Your Wallet

Link your USDT wallet securely. We support TRC-20, BEP-20, and ERC-20 networks via MetaMask, Trust Wallet, and more.

02

Scan UPI QR

Scan any UPI QR code or enter a UPI ID. Pay any person or merchant across India's UPI network.

03

Verify with 3 Random Characters

The system asks for the value of 3 random characters from your Fluxpay Verification Card. Enter them correctly and the smart contract triggers your wallet. Enter wrong and the transaction is declined instantly.

04

Pay Using Crypto

Your crypto is instantly converted at the live Google exchange rate via smart contract-based settlement.

05

Recipient Gets INR

The recipient -- whether a person or merchant -- receives INR directly to their UPI account. Seamless, instant, and fully compliant.
